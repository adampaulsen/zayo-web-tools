About Business Days

Business days exclude weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and major US federal holidays. This calculator automatically adjusts for:

  • New Year's Day
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Day
  • Presidents' Day
  • Memorial Day
  • Independence Day
  • Labor Day
  • Columbus Day
  • Veterans Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Christmas Day

Weekend & Holiday Handling

Smart Start Date: If you select a weekend or holiday as your start date, the calculator automatically uses the next business day instead. This ensures your calculations always start from a valid business day.

Example: If today is Saturday and you want "5 business days from today," the tool will calculate from Monday (the next business day) and give you the result for Friday.
